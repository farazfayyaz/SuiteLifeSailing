Tampa, Florida to Bahamas

Duration: April 2020, 5 day trip.

Trip Highlights

  1. Day 1 (Tampa): Florida Aquarium home to over 20,000 of aquatic plants and animals, Outdoor Channelside Plaza.
  2. Day 2 (Cruising) : On the ship there is a rock climbing wall, Mini golf course, restaurants, and pools.
  3. Day 3 (George Town, Grand Cayman): Swim with Turtles, Stroll the seven mile beach, 18 hole golf course, Swim with stingray, Enjoy local cuisine such as shellfish and rum.

San Juan, Puerto Rico to Southern Caribbean

Duration: July 2019, 6 day trip.

Trip Highlights

  1. Day 1: Cruising : On the ship there are rock climbing walls, pools, arcades, restaurants, and bars
  2. Day 2: ST. Johns, Antigua. : Hunt for history by visiting two restored windmill towers that were from the 1600s, visit Shirley Lookout which is a former military base to see the best views on the island, enjoy local cuisine where British, Creole, and West Indian flavors come together.
  3. Day 3: Castries St. Lucia: Visit the Sulphur Springs and enjoy mud baths, Visit Margiot Bay which is one of the top beaches in the world
