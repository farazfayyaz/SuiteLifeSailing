Alaska from Vancouver

Duration: April 2020, 7 day trip.

Trip Highlights

  1. Day 1(vancouver depart 4:00 pm:)- park 10% bigger than central park, snowboarding and surfing in the same day. It’s part city part beach (Kitsilano beach).
  2. Day 3 (cruise tracy arm fjord) narrow sea passage between two incredibly huge glaciers. Cruise through icy sculptures and sparkling waterfalls.
  3. Day 6 (ketchikan arrive 8:00 am depart 4:00 pm)- explore the outdoors with forests, creeks, fishing and mountain climbing .

Glacier Bay from Seattle

Duration: May 2019, 8 day trip.

Trip Highlights

  1. Day 1 (Seattle depart 4:00 pm)-major city with a small town vibe, food architecture and music.
  2. Day 3 (Juneau arrive 2:00 pm depart 11:00 pm)-kayaking, fishing, gold mining, a gorgeous adventure.
  3. Day 5 (cruise glacier bay) a protected national park spread across millions of acers. Majorly impressive views from the ship and tons of amazing facts and history about glacier bay.

Alaska from San Francisco

Duration: May 2020, 10 day trip.

Trip Highlights

  1. Day 1 (san francisco depart: 4:00 pm)- beautiful weather, scenery, food and culture. Visit the Alcatraz and golden gate.
  2. Day 5 (skagway: arrive 7:00 am-depart 9:00 pm)- historical wonder. Visit old saloons and relive the final chapter of the wild west days. Stroll along the boulevards to bring you back to the 1890’s Klondike gold rush days and go for the gold.
  3. Day 6 (Icy Strait Point: arrive 7:00 am-depart 9:00 pm)- tour the 1900s cannery, explore the world of native neighbors Tlingits and live the highlife with the world’s longest zip-line.
